Coaching with Tarot Cards

Need help making a decision? Not sure how to approach a situation? Do you know where you want to go, but you’re not sure how to get there?


Tarot gives you new insight and helps you see things from a different perspective. It helps you tap into your intuition for guidance.


During a session, we'll combine coaching principles with Tarot reading.

Tarot cards are a mirror. What you see in the images, reflects your (often subconscious) thoughts, emotions and desires.


Tarot readings bring deep insights and guidance, helping you gain clarity, make decisions, and navigate life's challenges.


Tarot coaching helps you align your goals with what you truly want and need. Not what you think you want, or should want.

Some questions you can explore during a session:


  • What should I focus on right now?
  • What steps do I need to take to achieve my goal?
  • What is holding me back from reaching my goal?
  • What do I need to know about a situation?

It's a great tool for reflection and growth that has helped me a lot and I'd love to share it with you. Curious to give it a try?
